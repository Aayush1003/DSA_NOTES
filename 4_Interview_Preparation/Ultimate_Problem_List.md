## Strategy: 
1. **Breadth First:** Solve 1-2 problems from each pattern first to understand the variety.
2. **Depth Second:** Dive into "Hard" problems once you feel comfortable with the "Medium" ones for a specific pattern.
3. **Timed Practice:** Use a timer (20m for Easy, 40m for Medium) to simulate interview pressure.
