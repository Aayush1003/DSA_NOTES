# Spaced Repetition Master Tracker

The secret to passing a top-tier interview isn't solving 500 problems—it's **remembering** the 100 patterns you already solved. Use this log.

## The Revision Schedule (Rule of 1-7-30)
- **Day 0:** Solve the problem.
- **Day 1:** Re-solve it (or trace it on paper).
- **Day 7:** Re-solve it without looking at previous code.
- **Day 30:** Re-solve it to ensure it's in long-term memory.

## How to use this:
1. Every time you solve a problem from the `Ultimate_Problem_List.md`, add it here.
2. If you struggle during a review, reset the timer (treat it as Day 0).
3. Focus on "Confidence 1-3" problems more than "Confidence 5" ones.

## The "Active Recall" Challenge
Before opening your code for a review:
1. Close your eyes.
2. Can you visualize the **state transition** (for DP) or the **pointer movement** (for Two Pointers)?
3. If you can't visualize it, you don't know it.
